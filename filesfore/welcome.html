I get tired of reading predictable books. I absolutely hate when I'm reading chapter 5 of a novel, and I can already see where the story is going to end. I hate it even more when I can tell the overall ending to a romance! So. . . where do I go from there? I spend my free time checking out romances and reccomending them to whomever sees this site.
If you like this site, please find whatever free time you have to suggest a book of your own!
